Q: What is a community?
------
A: This term describes all the living and nonliving things interacting in a particular area.
Q: What is an ecosystem?
------
A: This is something -- like food, water, space, or disease -- that causes a population to stop growing or to decrease in size.
Q: What is a limiting factor?
------
A: This term names the largest population size that an environment can support over time.
