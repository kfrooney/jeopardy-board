Q: What is a neuron?
------
A: This tiny gap between two neurons is where a signal is chemically passed from one neuron to the next.
Q: What is a synapse?
------
A: This type of fast, automatic, involuntary response to a stimulus does not require the brain to process it first.
Q: What is a reflex?
------
A: This control system works like a thermostat, reversing a change in the body to bring it back toward a stable, normal level.
