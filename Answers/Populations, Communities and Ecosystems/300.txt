Q: What is succession?
------
A: This type of succession occurs in an area where no soil or organisms previously existed, such as on bare rock after a volcanic eruption.
Q: What is primary succession?
------
A: This type of succession occurs in an area where the ecosystem was disturbed, but soil and organisms already exist, such as after a forest fire.
Q: What is secondary succession?
------
A: These are the first species, often mosses and lichens carried in by wind, to populate a new area during succession.
