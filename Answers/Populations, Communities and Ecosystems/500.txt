Q: What are ecosystem services?
------
A: Unlike conservation, which focuses on using less of a resource, this term describes an ecosystem's ability to maintain its biodiversity and production indefinitely.
Q: What is sustainability?
------
A: This practice involves actively repairing a degraded or destroyed ecosystem and protecting it from further damage, going a step further than simply conserving what remains.
Q: What is ecological restoration?
------
A: This is the practice of using less of a resource so that it lasts longer.
