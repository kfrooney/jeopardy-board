Q: What is an allele?
------
A: A trait controlled by this type of allele will always be expressed if at least one copy is present.
Q: What is dominant?
------
A: This inheritance pattern produces a blended trait, such as pink flowers from one red and one white parent allele.
Q: What is incomplete dominance?
------
A: This inheritance pattern, like human blood type AB, occurs when both alleles are fully and separately expressed at the same time.
