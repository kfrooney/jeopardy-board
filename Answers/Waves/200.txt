Q: What is a transverse wave?
------
A: In this type of wave, the medium moves back and forth in the same direction the wave travels, like a coiled spring being pushed and pulled.
Q: What is a longitudinal wave?
------
A: This is the maximum distance a wave's medium moves from its rest position, and it determines how much energy the wave carries.
Q: What is amplitude?
------
A: This is the distance between one point on a wave and the identical point on the next wave, such as crest to crest.
